  Follow the guidelines below and you should pass your test with no problems.

1. Emergency Stop

  1. Signal to the right.
  2. Slow down.
  3. Gradually pull the car over to the right side of the curb & stop completely.
  4. Check your right shoulder, making sure you are legally allowed to perform the emergency stop.
  5. Put the gear in the park position, & park.
  6. Pull up or turn on the handbrake.
  7. Press the emergency stop button to turn it on.
