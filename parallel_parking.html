3. Parallel Parking

  1. Signal to the right & slow down.
  2. Gradually approach the left side of the parked car, keeping approximately 1m away until you see the taillight of the parked car in your small triangle window (at the back of your car).
  3. Stop the car, putting the gear in reverse.
  4. Turn the wheels one full circle to the right.
  5. Release the brakes, letting the car move until you see the same taillight of the parked car behind the inner corner of the right mirror - & stop the car completely. Hold the brakes & turn the steering wheel completely to the left. Release the brakes.
  6. Let the car move until it becomes directly behind the parked car - in line with the car, facing in the same direction.
  7. Turn the wheels twice to the right (bring the white tape on the steering wheel to the top two times).
  8. Move the car either backwards or forwards, until you see the tires of the parked car on the ground.
  9. Put the gear on park, & turn the hand brake either up or on.
