12. Reverse Parking

  1. Keep the car straight in the centre of the road.
  2. Signal to the right.
  3. Line your shoulders up with the 3rd quarter of the second parking spot away.
  4. Put the gear in reverse, holding the brakes.
  5. Turn the steering wheels completely to the right.
  6. Release the brakes.
  7. Allow the car to move backwards. As soon as it becomes in line with the desired parking spot, turn the wheels twice to the left (bring the white tape at the top two times).
  8. Stop the car, place the gear in the parking position, turn the hand brake up or on.

13. Changing Lanes

  1. Check your shoulders & blind spots for the side you would like to switch to.
  2. Check the centre mirror for the cars behind you, making sure they are clearly visible in your mirror.
  3. Signal towards the side you want to switch to.
  4. Keep driving for approximately 5 seconds, gradually approaching the centre of the two lanes.
  5. If it is safe and clear to merge onto the desired lane, proceed with the same speed of the traffic or slightly more, according to the conditions of the traffic.
  6. Turn off your signal when you are in the desired lane.
