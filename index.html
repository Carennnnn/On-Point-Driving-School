KEY STEPS TO PASSING YOUR G2 & G TEST

Before you operate a car, you must pay careful attention to these 5 points. These are the main points that can decide whether you pass or fail your test.

  1. The gear must be in the right position (drive, reverse, or park).
  2. You must signal in the correct direction (right or left).
  3. The handbrake must be down or off while driving & must be on up or on while parked.
  4. You must look for safety (check the left mirrors for the left side, check the centre mirrors for the back, check the right mirrors for the right side, & shoulders for the blind spots) - if it is safe & clear, proceed to drive.
  5. When you start to drive, turn off the signal.
