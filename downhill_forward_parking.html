10. Downhill Parking With the Curb

  1. Signal to the right.
  2. Slow down.
  3. Gradually pull car over to the right side of the curb.
  4. Stop the car completely, close to the side of the curb, between 6in - 1ft away.
  5. Keeping the car in drive, turn the wheels all the way to the right.
  6. Slowly move the car to the right until you touch the curb.
  7. Stop the car, putting the gear in the park position with the hand brake up or on.

11. Forward Parking

  1. Keep the car straight in the centre of the road.
  2. Line your shoulder up with the middle of the parking spot before the spot that you wish to park in.
  3. Allow the car to move while turning the steering wheels completely to the left, until you are straight in line with the desired parking spot.
  4. Turn the wheel twice (bring white tape to the top of the steering wheel two times).
  5. Bring the car inside the spot, stopping before the end of the spot.
  6. Stop the car, putting the gear in the park position & pull up or turn on the handbrake.
