2. Three Point Turn

  1. Signal to the right.
  2. Slow down.
  3. Gradually pull the car over to the right side of the curb & stop completely. Hold the brake & keep the gear in drive.
  4. Check your right shoulder, making sure you are legally allowed to perform the three-point turn.
  5. Signal to the left.
  6. Do a complete safety check (check the left mirror for the left side, check the centre mirror for the back, check the right mirrors for the right side, & check both shoulders for blind spots).
  7. Release the brakes, moving the car while turning the wheels to the left, & go to the left side of the road (to the other curb, stopping the car before you hit it).
  8. Put the gear in reverse.
  9. Signal to the right.
  10. Do a complete safety check (check the left mirror for the left side, check the centre mirror for the back, check the right mirrors for the right side, & check both shoulders for blind spots).
  11. Release the brakes.
  12. Proceed back the curb, stopping the car before you hit it.
  13. Put the gear on drive.
  14. Signal to the left.
  15. Do a complete safety check (check the left mirror for the left side, check the centre mirror for the back, check the right mirrors for the right side, & check both shoulders for blind spots).
  16. Accelerate & proceed to drive.
