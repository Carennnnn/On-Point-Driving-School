4. Turning Right at a Stop Sign

  1. Approach the stop sign, & stop the car 1 - 2ft completely before the stop lane for three seconds.
  2. Check your left shoulder for the left side of the road & check your right shoulder for blind spots.
  3. If it is clear & safe, accelerate & proceed to drive.

5. Turning Right at a Red Light

  1. Stop 1 - 2ft completely before the stop lane.
  2. Check your left shoulder for oncoming traffic & pedestrians, & check your right shoulder for blind spots.
  3. If it is safe & clear, accelerate & proceed to turn right.

Note: If the left side is not clear & safe, do not turn right on the red light.
Make sure that you NEVER block the crosswalk for pedestrians.
